Privacy Policy - QR Generator

We value your privacy. This application is designed to function without collecting or storing personal information from users.

Information We Collect

The app may collect the following types of data:

  • QR Generation Input: The text or URL you enter to generate a QR code is processed locally on your device. It is never uploaded or stored on any server.
  • Network Scanning: If you use the Wi-Fi device scanning feature, the app may detect connected devices on your local network.
    ✔ This information is accessed only on your device and is NOT transmitted, stored, or shared.
  • Device Information (Optional): Anonymous data such as device model or OS version may be collected only for app performance analytics.

How We Use the Information

  • Generating QR codes locally on your device.
  • Scanning and listing Wi-Fi devices available on your local network.
  • Improving app performance and fixing bugs through anonymous analytics.

Data Sharing

We do not share, sell, or transfer any type of personal data or network information to any third party.

Security

All processing happens locally on your device. No data is transmitted externally, ensuring maximum privacy and security.
